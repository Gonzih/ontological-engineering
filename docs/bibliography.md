# Bibliography

This bibliography tracks the academic and standards references currently grounding the repository. The purpose is not exhaustive citation. It is to preserve a source map for the fields we are synthesizing under the ontological engineering lens.

## Knowledge Engineering

- Schreiber, Akkermans, Anjewierden, de Hoog, Shadbolt, Van de Velde, and Wielinga. _Knowledge Engineering and Management: The CommonKADS Methodology_. MIT Press. https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262193009/knowledge-engineering-and-management/

## Knowledge Representation And Reasoning

- Brachman and Levesque. _Knowledge Representation and Reasoning_. Morgan Kaufmann. https://www.sciencedirect.com/book/monograph/9781558609327/knowledge-representation-and-reasoning

- Suarez-Figueroa, Gomez-Perez, and Fernandez-Lopez. "The NeOn Methodology framework: A scenario-based methodology for ontology development." https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.3233/AO-150145
